My Datastory

Writing With And For Digital Media

In this final project, I reflect on each of my projects and develop a narrative about what I have learned about data and information.

The Dataviz Project

My first project involved collecting, organizing and creating a set of data in Google Sheets for 7 days about one of my everyday digital habits. Specifically, I tracked the type of playlist I listened to on Spotify. The three families of playlists I tracked were: Daily Mixes - which were generated to match my preferences; Artist Radios - which were collections of music similar to selected artists; and Specifically Selected - which were songs I selected individually outside of any playlist. In addition to tracking the type of playlist, I also recorded how much attention I was giving to the music on a scale from 1 to 5, and how many songs I skipped.

The Explained Video Project

In my Explained Video project, my team explored possible breaches of ethics with regards to the use of cryptocurrencies. While there are some desirable attributes of CC use - such as an immutable record of transactions and confidentiality - they can be exploited for nefarious purposes. Many governments are in the process of deciding how they will regulate CC's.
